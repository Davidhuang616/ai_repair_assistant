How to replace the boost button
1. You will need the following tools.

Allen key 2.5mm

Buy

Round nose pliers

Buy

Boost Button

Buy
2. You can follow the steps below to replace the new boost button.

(move the cursor over each step to see a bigger image)

Remember to turn off the power first.

Step 1

Turn off the power.

Step 2

Unscrew the button using an 2.5mm Allen key.

Step 3

Gently pull the button up to avoid disconnection
from the connector. Use tools if necessary.

Step 4

Use tweezers to hold the connector in place, then
gently remove the button from the connector.

Step 5

Use tweezers to secure the connector, then align
the new button contact with the connector and gently
insert it into the connector.

Step 6

Turn on the power and wait until the screen lights up.

Step 7

Press the button to test if it can change the boost
level on the screen.

Step 8

Ensure the flat surface on the side of the button
faces the bolt and then put the button back into
the hole.

Step 9

Fasten the bolt using an 2.5mm Allen key.

Step 10

Congratulations! You replaced the button successfully.
